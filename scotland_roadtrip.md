### **Day 5: Wednesday, Dec 24 (Skye & Ness - Christmas Eve)**
*Goal: The "Highland Loop" - Eilean Donan, Skye Bridge & Loch Ness.*
üìç **[CLICK HERE to open Day 5 Route Map](https://maps.google.com/?cid=9463387316565041206&g_mp=Cidnb29nbGUubWFwcy5wbGFjZXMudjEuUGxhY2VzLlNlYXJjaFRleHQ8)**

* **08:30 | Depart Onich**
    * *Drive:* North on A82 -> A87 (The "Road to the Isles").

    * *Stop:* 45 mins. The most photographed castle in Scotland. (Outside/Coffee shop).
* **11:00 | Skye Bridge**
    * *Drive:* Cross the bridge to **Kyleakin** (Skye). Touch the island soil!
    * *Photo:* View back towards the mainland mountains.
* **12:00 | Drive to Loch Ness**
    * *Route:* A87 -> A887 -> A82 (Invermoriston).
* **13:30 | Fort Augustus (Loch Ness)**
    * *Stop:* 1 hour. See the locks and the view down Loch Ness.
    * *Lunch:* Grab food in Fort Augustus.
* **15:00 | Commando Memorial**
    * *Stop:* 15 mins. Famous statue with views of Ben Nevis.
* **16:00 | Arrive Fort William**
    * *Accommodation:* **Muthu Fort William Hotel** (Booked).
* **18:00 | Christmas Eve Dinner**
    * *Action:* Book table at hotel or **The Geographer** / **Crannog** in town.
